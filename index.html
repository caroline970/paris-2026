Part 2 Must-dos & little joys

✨ See the Eiffel Tower sparkle

Every night it glitters for five minutes on the hour — and in midsummer the first show is around 11 PM. Your Thursday boat glides right past it. To catch the twinkle another night, watch from Pont de Bir-Hakeim, where the arches frame the tower and the crowds thin out.

Pont de Bir-Hakeim, 75015 Paris

🥐 The croissant crawl

Three worth every calorie:

  • Tout Autour du Pain — in the Marais, steps from home base. Your everyday spot.134 Rue de Turenne, 75003 Paris
  • La Maison d'Isabelle — two minutes from Notre-Dame. Famously the best croissant in the city, just €1.40 — grab one Sunday around Mass.47ter Boulevard Saint-Germain, 75005 Paris
  • Du Pain et des Idées — by the Canal Saint-Martin. A 24-hour-rise legend; pair it with a canal walk.34 Rue Yves Toudic, 75010 Paris

🚶 Long walks, the right way

You love to walk — so do it when Paris is coolest: before noon or after 6 PM, never the 2 PM heat. The best routes:

  • Marais → Île Saint-Louis → Notre-Dame along the river — your Sunday morning.Île Saint-Louis, 75004 Paris
  • Berges de Seine, the car-free riverbank, at golden hour.Port de Solférino, 75007 Paris
  • Canal Saint-Martin — leafy, local, and croissant-adjacent.Quai de Valmy, 75010 Paris

🍫 One sweet splurge

A box of macarons from Pierre Hermé to split on a bench in the Place des Vosges. That's the whole activity.

Pierre Hermé: 72 Rue Bonaparte, 75006 · Place des Vosges, 75004
